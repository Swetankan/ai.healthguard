AI Health Guard

AI Health Guard is a concept that combines the capabilities of artificial intelligence(AI) with healthcare to enhance the quality and efficiency of medical services.

Health Guard.ai offers symptom-based disease analysis, with accurate health information for informed decision-making

Users input symptoms, receive potential diagnoses, easing worries and aiding in treatment decisions.

"Empower yourself with knowledge, for with understanding comes the strength to conquer any challenge that life may throw your way."

Tuberculosis (TB)

  • Fatigue
  • coughing blood
  • Chills
  • Fever
  • Appetite Loss

Prevention

  • Stay home. Stay Safe
  • Isolate at home
  • Wear face masks
  • Ventilate the room.

Protective Measures

  • Get Vaccination
  • Proper Excecise
  • Take medications at the right time
  • Eat a balanced diet.
